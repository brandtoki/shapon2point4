Who I Am

Filmmaker & Entrepreneur

01

I'm Shapon — a filmmaker, entrepreneur, and creative mind based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. My work sits at the intersection of cinema, culture, and commerce, with a deep commitment to Bangladeshi storytelling and identity.

Through The Mrityukhudha Society, I direct short films and documentaries that draw from the rich visual language of 70s Bengali cinema. At Think King, I co-lead a 360° creative agency that helps brands find their authentic voice.

Khalnayak is a clothing brand born of cinema and cultural memory. And through A Modern Monk, I explore ideas at the crossroads of philosophy, learning, and creative living.
