The book that taught me that poetry and image are the same thing — that a frame is a stanza.

How we look at images is never neutral. This book changed my relationship to the camera forever.
Story

The architecture of narrative — not as formula, but as deep structure that mirrors how humans make meaning.

The work that gave The Mrityukhudha Society its name. A reminder that art can be both fierce and tender.
